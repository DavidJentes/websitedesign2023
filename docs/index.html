Helping others through troubling times

Here at The Chereb Foundation, we help others during hard times in their life. Whether it's debt, loss of family members or friends, or whatever else it may be, we're here to help.

Donate

Send a check to 399 Glenwood Dr. Wisconsin Rapids, WI 54494 to donate. An online solution is coming soon!

How else can I help?

You can help through prayer, or attending one of our events! See a list of events on our calendar page!

Get help

On our Contact Us page, we have a link to send us an email for help. You can also call us at (800) 555-2368.

I want to volunteer!

You can email us to become a volunteer on our Contact Us page. This is the only way to sign up.
